# Afritok - User Guide

**Purpose:** Afritok is the social video platform designed for African creators to upload, share, and monetize their short-form video content directly from their country in their local currency.

**Access:** Public platform with login required for creators. Sign in to upload videos, like content, and access your monetization dashboard.

---

## Powered by Manus

Afritok is built on a cutting-edge tech stack:

- **Frontend:** React 19 + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS 4 with custom African-inspired color palette (vibrant orange, green, red, and gold)
- **Backend:** Express 4 + tRPC 11 for type-safe API communication
- **Database:** MySQL with Drizzle ORM for robust data management
- **Authentication:** Manus OAuth for secure user authentication
- **Deployment:** Auto-scaling infrastructure with global CDN for lightning-fast video delivery across Africa

## Using Your Website

### 1. Explore the Feed

When you log in, you're taken to the **Feed** page where you can scroll through videos from creators across Africa. Use the up/down arrows or scroll to navigate through an infinite stream of content. Each video displays the creator's engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares, views).

### 2. Interact with Videos

For each video you see, you can:

- **Like:** Click the heart icon on the right side to like a video. Your like is instantly recorded and contributes to the creator's engagement.
- **Comment:** Click the comment bubble to leave feedback on videos (feature coming soon for full comment UI).
- **Share:** Click the share icon to share videos with others outside the platform.

### 3. Visit Creator Profiles

Click on any creator's name or profile picture to visit their profile page. Here you can:

- See all their uploaded videos in a grid layout
- View their follower and following counts
- Click "Follow" to stay updated with their latest content
